About

Hi! I'm Adrien Manaloto, a BSIT student passionate about video editing and graphic design. I love creating visuals that tell stories — from short-form videos and motion graphics to eye-catching designs that bring ideas to life. My freelance work has sharpened my creativity, attention to detail, and storytelling skills, which I now carry into my growing interest in basic web design and front-end development. I'm continuously learning, exploring new tools, and finding ways to combine my design background with web development to craft engaging digital experiences. Beyond technical skills, I value collaboration and clear communication, as I believe the best results come from working closely with clients or teammates to bring their vision to life. My goal is to continue growing as a creative professional, building a portfolio that reflects not only my skills but also my passion for design, storytelling, and creating meaningful digital experiences.
